关于德裕新About Deyuxin

中山市德裕新塑胶制品有限公司成立于2012年，是一家集吸塑与折盒产品研发、设计、制造与销售为一体的现代化生产加工型企业。Founded in 2012, Zhongshan Deyuxin Plastics Co., Ltd. is an integrated R&D, design, manufacturing, and sales enterprise specializing in blister and folding box packaging.

我们位于广东省中山市坦洲镇，毗邻珠海、澳门，东临深圳、东莞，地理位置优越，物流便捷，可快速响应国内外客户需求。Located in Tanzhou Town, Zhongshan — adjacent to Zhuhai and Macau, close to Shenzhen and Dongguan — we offer excellent logistics to serve domestic and international clients swiftly.
