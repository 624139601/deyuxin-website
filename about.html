关于德裕新About Deyuxin

15年吸塑行业深耕，以品质和创新为核心，为全球客户提供卓越的塑胶包装解决方案15 years of blister packaging expertise — quality and innovation at our core, serving clients worldwide

我们的故事Our Story

德裕新成立于2012年，是一家集吸塑与折盒产品研发、设计、制造与销售为一体的现代化生产加工型企业。Deyuxin was founded in 2012 as an integrated R&D, design, manufacturing, and sales enterprise specializing in blister and folding box packaging.

在吸塑领域潜心钻研十五年，所有产品均符合欧洲、美国、日本、韩国包装测试性能及环保标准，赢得了国内外众多知名品牌企业的长期信赖与合作。With 15 years of focused expertise in blister packaging, all products comply with EU, US, Japan, and Korea environmental and performance standards — earning the long-term trust of renowned brands domestically and internationally.

我们位于广东省中山市坦洲镇，毗邻珠海、澳门，东临深圳、东莞，凭借优越的地理位置与完善的物流体系，能够快速响应国内外客户需求。Located in Tanzhou Town, Zhongshan — adjacent to Zhuhai and Macau, close to Shenzhen and Dongguan — our prime location and robust logistics enable rapid response to clients worldwide.

发展历程Company Milestones

十五年砥砺前行，每一步都是积淀Fifteen years of progress — every step is a foundation

2012

公司成立Company Founded

中山市德裕新塑胶制品有限公司正式成立，专注吸塑包装领域。Zhongshan Deyuxin Plastics Co., Ltd. established, focusing on blister packaging.

2015

工厂扩建Factory Expansion

扩建至6000㎡现代化厂房，引进先进吸塑设备，产能大幅提升。Expanded to 6,000㎡ modern facility with advanced thermoforming equipment, significantly boosting capacity.

2018

ISO认证ISO Certification

通过ISO 9001质量管理体系认证，产品品质与管理水平全面提升。Achieved ISO 9001 Quality Management System certification, elevating product quality and management standards.

2021

开拓国际市场International Expansion

产品出口欧美、日韩等地，建立稳固的国际合作伙伴网络。Products exported to Europe, USA, Japan, and Korea, establishing a solid international partner network.

2026

数字化升级Digital Transformation

建立全新企业官网，推进数字化营销，进一步拓展国内外市场。Launched new corporate website, advancing digital marketing to further expand domestic and international markets.
